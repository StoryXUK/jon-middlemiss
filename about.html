JONATHAN MIDDLEMISS

Jonathan Middlemiss was a renowned artist known for his exceptional skills in creating intricate ceramic sculptures which integrate themes of geometry, ecology, and landscape. His art is inspired by nature, and he often used natural materials such as wood, stone, and metal in his works. He was born in 1949 and passed away in 2021, leaving behind an impressive collection of art that continues to inspire and captivate art enthusiasts worldwide.

“You must”

The day before his passing he gifted all images and rights of his lifetime’s work to his daughter, with the instructions to use them and say “My Dad did that”. Jonathan always said that the art is his children’s legacy, Serena will be working closely with her brother Julian are committed to preserving this legacy with his life’s philosophy that inspired new perspectives in art and personal transformation.

With a background in design and a lifetime of understanding her father's work, Serena is the one person suited to organizing exhibitions, authenticating, and promoting her father's artwork. She currently working with her brother Julian to catalogue and document Jonathan's extensive collection of art and writing his biography to ensure that his complete story is preserved and accessible to future generations.
