Jonathan Middlemiss

Ceramic Artist.

Jonathan Middlemiss was a renowned artist known for his exceptional skills in creating intricate ceramic sculptures which integrates themes of geometry, ecology, and landscape. His art is inspired by nature, and he often used natural materials such as wood, stone, and metal in his works. He was born in 1949 and passed away in 2021, leaving behind an impressive collection of art that continues to inspire and captivate art enthusiasts worldwide.
