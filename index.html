Jonathan Middlemiss

Middlemiss Art

Welcome to the website of the late artist, Jonathan Middlemiss. This site is dedicated to showcasing his incredible ceramic artwork and honoring his memory.

Jonathan Middlemiss was a renowned artist who dedicated his life to creating unique and stunning pieces of ceramics. His work has been exhibited in galleries and museums around the world, and his legacy continues to inspire future generations of artists.

On this website, you'll find a collection of Jonathan's most iconic ceramic pieces, each one a testament to his incredible talent and artistic vision. From delicate vessels to bold and expressive sculptures, each piece tells a story and invites you to explore the depth and beauty of Jonathan's work.

We hope that this website will serve as a lasting tribute to Jonathan and his incredible contributions to the world of art. Please take some time to explore the collection and experience the magic of Jonathan Middlemiss's ceramics for yourself.
